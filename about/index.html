About Me

   Hey! My name is Nooh Alavi, and I'm just a kid who likes coding and game development! I made this site as a place to post my projects, videos, tutorials, devlogs and thoughts.

   I started coding in fourth grade, when I picked up a book on HTML and CSS in my school book fair. My dream since I was a kid was making my own video games, and that dream could finally come true - but not yet. I soon discovered HTML wasnt.. well... ideal for making video games. However, that book inspired me to learn other coding languages.

   Soon, I learned Python and JavaScript, and made a few simple games. I remember making clones of games such as Snake, Space Invaders, etc, and a few other unoriginal ideas, such as an infinite runner (with terrible collision detection), and a game where you had to avoid falling objects from the sky (at that point, I had learned the AABB algorithm, and could actually implement proper collision detection).

   And then, I found out about game engines, which was quite literally game changing for me. I tried out a couple, including Unity, Phaser.js, Love2D, and even explored some fantasy consoles, but in the end I stuck with Godot, a decision I don't regret.

   Now, I make most of my games using Godot with the C# scripting language, though I always love learning and trying out new languages and tools! I post devlogs, tutorials and my discoveries on my youtube channel.

   But coding and gamedev aren't my only hobbies! I love playing many different video games with friends, including Minecraft, Among Us and chess, and I stream them on my twitch channel. In fact, I'm even a lichess streamer!
